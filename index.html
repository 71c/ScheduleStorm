About Schedule Storm

What is Schedule Storm?

Schedule Storm is a multi-university schedule generator that produces schedules live with the courses you add to My Courses. With it you can add specific classes or general courses and give preference weighting based on what you would want your schedule to look like. We have also included Rate My Professor ratings for all professors that have such a rating. You can view these beside the professors name in brackets.

How Do You Use Schedule Storm?

At the top of the page in the right corner there is a envelope symbol that looks like . Click on this and it will go through a tutorial on how to use the site.

Who developed Schedule Storm?

Schedule Storm was developed by Perplex(Front-End) and Step7750(Back-end). We are both currently university students who were fed up with terrible schedule builders that our universities provided. You can contact us by leaving a comment on either of our steam profiles, by adding us on steam and sending us a message, or by leaving an issue on the Github page
